Are You Ready for Title 24?

Starting January 1st, 2014, the California Energy Commission (CEC) will enforce its updated Title 24 energy verification standards to new construction and retrofits.
 
The updated regulations were designed for contractors and end users with commercial and industrial projects. Let All Industrial Electric Supply help you fulfill California's requirements with Leviton's metering solutions.
