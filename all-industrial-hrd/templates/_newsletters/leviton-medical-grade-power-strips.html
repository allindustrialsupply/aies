Introducing Leviton's Medical Grade Power Strips

The ultimate protection for sensitive electronic equipment

All Industrial Electric Supply is pleased to announce we will carry Leviton's medical grade power strips. These are designed explicitly for use in health care facilities, including patient care areas as defined by NEC Article 517.

Features

  • Conforms to UL 60601-1, UL 60950-1 and UL 1363A
  • Available in 15- and 20-amp configurations
  • UL 1449 3rd edition compliant, providing the ultimate protection against voltage surges
  • Stock configurations include four- & six-outlet strips with 7-foot & 15-foot cord lengths
  • Two-outlet configurations, and other options, available by special order
  • Satisfy electrical safety requirements specified in NFPA 99: Health Care Facilities Code
