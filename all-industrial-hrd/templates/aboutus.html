About Us


All Industrial Electric Supply (AIES) has served the critical need for a superior industrial, contractor, nationwide, and commercial electrical distributor since its inception in 2004. When it comes to electrical supplies, AIES is unsurpassed.


Save time, money and make your job easier

AIES brings a combination of price, inventory, and top quality service to offer you unparalleled value for your electrical supply purchases.


We have a large inventory

AIES has close to $7.5 million in local inventory and is a stocking distributor for multiple vendors.


We have unrivaled product access

Through the AIES network of companies, AIES can source almost any part at preferred prices - even parts that are obsolete.


Experience high touch service

With AIES, you get the personal attention of an assigned account manager with a direct line. Service is available in the evening and on weekends.

AIES works with you to achieve extra cost savings by consolidating purchases of major manufacturers. If you are new to AIES, we encourage you to visit and discover just how committed AIES is to serving your electrical needs.

To help keep you aware of ever-changing technologies and safety compliance issues, AIES partners with manufacturers to conduct training or site visits that aim to address your specific problems or needs.


Our prices are highly competitive

As part of the IMARK Group with over $1 billion of annual aggregate purchasing power, AIES benefits from preferred pricing and passes the savings to you.


We are your sole source for all of your electrical supplies

With exceptional pricing, product access, inventory, and service, you can save time and money by consolidating your electrical parts purchases. One point of contact. One purchase order. One solution.
